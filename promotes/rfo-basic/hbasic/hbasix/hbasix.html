hBasiX * eXperimental *


hBasiX (by humpty) is a fork of the hBasic Interpreter which is a fork of Basic!
originally  Rfo-Basic 01.91 (De Re Basic) for Android (by Paul Laughton).


hBasiX  =  hBasic  +  libGDX game engine


status: experimental

This side project arose out of curiosity of whether the libGDX could be compiled with hBasic.
Having achieved that, the future remains unconfirmed dependent on furthur results.

The current objective is for 2D sprites only.

You are welcome to try out whatever is below.
